How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

Three simple steps. No training needed. No complicated setup.

01
📷
Customer Scans QR or Books Online
Place your unique QR code at the entrance, reception, or share the link on social media. Customers scan or tap to join your queue from wherever they are.
02
🏡
They Wait Comfortably — Remotely
Instead of sitting in a crowded lobby, customers go grab a coffee, run errands, or relax at home. Your waiting room stays calm and clutter-free.
03
🔔
Notified the Moment It's Their Turn
FlowQueue automatically sends a WhatsApp or SMS alert when the customer is next. They arrive right on time — no guessing, no anxiety.
